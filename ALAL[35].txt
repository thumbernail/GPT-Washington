                            <h1>True education</h1>
</div>
                            

        <div class="lead">Trusted by schools and families for over 40 years</div>
<div class="lead"><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Education/allschool" style="font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18.2px; background-color: #ffffff;">The True All School program </a><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Education/allschool"></a></div>
<p><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Education/allschool">The True All School program </a>is the first course of its kind, mapped to the national curriculum. True is renowned for the quality of its content and delivery, which is why it has been trusted by schools and families for over 40 years. The All School program comprises a suite of sessions for children and teachers in primary school, secondary school and children with disability across all year levels.&nbsp;Education is also available to parents and carers.&nbsp;</p>
<hr />
<div class="contentEditor" id="ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_nestedAreaTwo_contentAreaZone_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_divContents"><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Education/multicultural" style="background-color: #ffffff; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18.2px;">Multicultural health&nbsp;</a><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: museo500; color: #985fa1;"><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Education/multicultural"></a></span></div>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 16.8px;"><span style="font-size: 14px;">Reproductive and sexual health services need to be culturally responsive. The Culturally Responsive Reproductive and Sexual Health Project builds capacity of health professionals and strengthens health institutions. It seeks to increase health literacy and service accessibility, to improve reproductive and sexual health outcomes for migrant and refugee populations.&nbsp;</span></p>
<hr />
<div class="contentEditor" id="ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_nestedAreaTwo_contentAreaZone_ContentPlaceHolderMainNoAjax_ctl00_ctl00_ctl00_divContents"><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Education/traffic-lights" style="background-color: #ffffff; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18.2px;">Traffic Lights</a><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: tahoma, geneva, verdana, sans-serif; color: #222222;"><a href="https://www.true.org.au/Relationships---sexuality/our-services"><span style="font-size: 11px;">&reg;</span></a></span></div>
<p>Traffic Lights&reg; <span style="font-size: 11px; font-family: tahoma, geneva, verdana, sans-serif;"><a style="background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 20px;"><span style="font-size: 11px;"></span></a></span>is trusted by schools, childcare centres, professionals and parents across the world to decode child sexual behaviour. The standard has been widely adopted because it provides an intuitive way to determine whether a child's behaviour is developmentally normative for their age. True provides a suite of training options and resources to help create shared language and understanding, often reducing stress, fear and over-reporting.</p>
